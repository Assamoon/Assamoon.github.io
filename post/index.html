Dataset

The game is famous and has catalyzed many communities. However, to limit the scope of the project, the data that will be used is from two sources. The original data comes from [Wikipedia][https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Wiki], which is the main source of this project. We extract 730 characters and 121 monsters from “The Witcher 3” and its DLCs (Downloadable content, is additional content created for an already released video game). Another dataset is from [Transcripts Fandom][https://transcripts.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher], which contains the dialog from TV series “The Witcher”.

Network

The network consists with 787 nodes and 1514 edges which contains all the creatures included both characters and bestiaries. Each character has different attributes, such as race, profession, nationality, etc. Monsters have eleven categories, such as Elementa, Hybrids, and their respective locations. Some characters are also belongs to bestiaries, that’s why the nodes is 787 rather than 730 + 121 = 851.

These graph shows a little more variety, but most of the nodes from this subset of nodes with a degree higher than 5 have a degree lower than around 50. Higher than that are just a handful of nodes. The main characters, Geralt and Yennefer takes account for the most links.
